Bio Magic Privacy Policy

Introduction

Bio Magic respects the privacy of its users and is committed to protecting their personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

When you use Bio Magic, we may collect certain information from you:

  • Personal Information: We may collect your name and email address when you contact us for support.
  • Usage Information: We may collect information about how you use the app, such as your interactions with the features and functionalities.
  • Device Information: We may collect information about your device, including the device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers.

How We Use Your Information

We may use the information we collect for various purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the app.
  • To improve and personalize your experience with the app.
  • To respond to your inquiries and provide support.
  • To analyze usage trends and understand how users interact with the app.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties without your consent. However, we may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • With service providers who assist us in providing and maintaining the app.
  • With law enforcement or government authorities if required by law or to protect our legal rights.
  • In connection with a business transaction, such as a merger or acquisition.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the information we collect from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the updated Privacy Policy will be effective when posted.
